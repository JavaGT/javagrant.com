Modular Storage Systems

If you are storing household items, books, games, or tools, it is almost always better to use an established modular system than to invent your own.

Why use a modular system?

  • Expandable: add units later as your needs change.
  • Standardised: cheap inserts and accessories fit reliably.
  • Widely available: easy to replace damaged units or buy more.

Kallax-style cube storage

The IKEA Kallax series is the most common example of this style. Bunnings sells a close equivalent under the Flexi Storage Clever Cube name.

Important: Kallax-compatible units are not the same size as cube systems sold by The Warehouse or Kmart. Inserts are not interchangeable. Kmart and The Warehouse cubes are much smaller.
